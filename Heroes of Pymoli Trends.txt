Heroes of Pymoli Commentary
Of the 576 game players, the vast majority are young males.
484 of the players are males which makes up 84%
45% of all players are between the ages of 20 and 24.
75% of all players are between the ages of 15 and 29.

It is difficult to determine trends from the data we have.
One cannot determine whether the game is increasing as a
whole and/or within the various segments.

Given the sharp increase in expendible income in high school
and college, the game owners should pay particular attention
to the younger plays as they grow into the sweetspot of the game.
